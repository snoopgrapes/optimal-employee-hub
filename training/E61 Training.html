Full Operating Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the entire operating procedure. Expand each section to see the detailed steps.

1. Preparation & Pre-Operation Checks

‚ñº
  • Complete all safety checks from the initial safety briefing.
  • Fill Solution Tank: Pour clean water and the correct ratio of cleaning solution into the tank. Do not overfill.
  • Check Recovery Tank: Ensure it is empty and the drain hose is sealed.
  • Install Brushes/Pads: Attach the correct brushes or pads for the floor type securely.
  • Lower Squeegee: Make sure the squeegee assembly is down and locked in its operating position.

2. Machine Operation

‚ñº
  • Turn ON the main power switch.
  • Engage the brush motor to start scrubbing.
  • Engage the vacuum motor to start water pickup.
  • Begin cleaning by moving forward at a steady walking pace.
  • Adjust the solution flow based on the level of dirt on the floor.
  • Overlap cleaning paths by a few inches for complete coverage.
  • Monitor the solution and recovery tank levels continuously. Refill or empty as needed.

3. Finishing & Post-Operation

‚ñº
  • Turn off the brush motor.
  • Continue forward for a few feet to allow the squeegee to pick up remaining water.
  • Turn off the vacuum motor, then the main power switch.
  • Raise the squeegee assembly to the transport position.
  • Proceed to the "Maintenance" section for required daily cleaning procedures.

Interactive Troubleshooting

Select an issue to see possible causes and solutions. For complex issues, consult the full manual or a supervisor.

Select an issue from the list to view solutions here.

Solution for: Machine Not Turning On

Possible Causes: Battery is flat, main power switch is off, or a fuse has blown.

Solutions: Check power connection, fully charge the battery, ensure the main power switch is ON, or check/replace the fuse as per the manual.

Solution for: Poor Water Pick-up

Possible Causes: Squeegee blades are worn/dirty, squeegee is poorly adjusted, recovery tank is full, vacuum hose is clogged, or the recovery tank lid is not sealed.

Solutions: Clean or replace squeegee blades, adjust the squeegee angle, empty the recovery tank, check the vacuum hose for blockages, and ensure the recovery tank lid is sealed tightly.

Solution for: No Solution Dispensed

Possible Causes: Solution tank is empty, solution flow valve is closed, or the solution filter is clogged.

Solutions: Refill the solution tank, open the solution flow valve, and clean the solution filter.

Solution for: Brushes Not Rotating

Possible Causes: Brush motor is off, brushes are jammed, or brushes are worn out.

Solutions: Turn on the brush motor, check for and remove any obstructions, and replace worn brushes.
