Optimal FM Casual Worker Policy

Your guide to working with us as a casual cleaning operative

Last updated: June 2023

Casual Work Arrangement

This is not an employment contract. Work is offered at our discretion with no obligation on either side.

Flexible Working

Each assignment is separate. You're free to accept or decline work offers as they come.

Pay & Benefits

£12.21/hour, paid monthly. Pro-rated holiday pay and statutory sick pay when eligible.

Welcome to Optimal FM

This policy explains the terms under which we engage casual workers for cleaning operations. Please read it carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities when working with us.

Key Points to Remember

  • This is a casual work arrangement, not an employment contract
  • We're not obligated to offer work, and you're not obligated to accept
  • Each assignment is treated as a separate engagement
  • You'll be paid £12.21 per hour for hours actually worked
  • You're entitled to pro-rated holiday pay

For You

  • Flexibility to accept or decline work
  • Clear pay rate and payment schedule
  • Protection under worker rights legislation

For Us

  • Right to offer work at our discretion
  • Flexibility to meet changing business needs
  • Clear terms for engagement

How Work is Arranged

Offer & Acceptance Process

We'll Contact You

When we have work available, we will contact you by telephone with details about the proposed appointment.

You Choose

There is no obligation for you to perform work when offered , and you are under no obligation to accept work offered by us.

If You Accept

Once you accept an appointment, you must notify the Company immediately if you cannot complete it for any reason.

Assignment Details

  • • Each offer of work you accept is a separate and severable arrangement, with no continuity between them.
  • • Work locations may vary across the UK.
  • • You may be responsible for arranging your own transport to the work site.
  • • Your hours of work will be agreed for each assignment.

Your Duties

  • • Your duties will be to carry out cleaning operations.
  • • You will follow the instructions of your supervisor/manager and report to them as notified.
  • • You may be required to carry out other duties to meet operational needs.
  • • Specific requirements will be explained at the start of each assignment.

Your Pay & Benefits

Pay Details

  • Hourly Rate: £12.21 (gross)
  • Payment Schedule: Monthly in arrears
  • Payment Date: Last working day of month
  • Pay Period: 16th to 15th of the month

Holiday Entitlement

  • Full-time Equivalent: 28 days/year (pro rata)
  • Your Entitlement: Pro-rated based on days worked
  • Holiday Year: 1st Jan - 31st Dec
  • Unused Holiday: Paid in lieu when assignment ends

Breaks During Work

You are entitled to a paid break of up to 30 minutes for every 6 hours worked in a day.
You are entitled to an unpaid break of up to 30 minutes for every 6 hours worked beyond your first 6 hours.

Unauthorised breaks may result in pay deductions.

Sick Pay

You are entitled to Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) if you meet the qualifying conditions.

If you are sick, you must notify the company at least 4 hours before your shift begins where practicable.

Your Responsibilities

Before Starting Work

  • • You are required to complete the information form and return it to head office at least one week before the engagement starts.
  • • You must provide documents proving your legal entitlement to work in the UK.
  • • You must inform the Company immediately if you cease to be legally entitled to work in the UK.

During Work

  • • You must comply with all Company rules, policies, and procedures.
  • • You must not use or disclose any confidential information about the Company's business.
  • • All Company property must be returned upon request or at the end of an appointment.

Important Notice: Gross Misconduct

Examples of gross misconduct include dishonesty, theft, fighting, misuse of drugs or alcohol, or any acts that might bring the Company into disrepute. Such conduct may result in immediate termination of the contract.

Ending the Arrangement

By You

  • You can stop being considered for work at any time by informing Head Office or your Line Manager.

By Us

  • We may remove you from our database if you cannot accept an appointment on 3 consecutive occasions or do not work for 4 consecutive weeks.
  • We may terminate the contract immediately for a serious breach of terms or gross misconduct.
  • We may terminate this contract if we vary the terms on which we engage casual workers.

After Termination

  • You will be paid for all work completed up to the date of termination.
  • Payment will be made in lieu of any accrued but untaken holiday.
  • You must return all Company property at the end of an appointment.

Policy Acknowledgement

By working with Optimal FM as a casual worker, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
